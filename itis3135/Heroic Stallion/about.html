Heroic Stallion — About

Heroic Stallion started with a messy desk and a love of bold, minimalist designs. We make pocket-sized tools that keep your cables neat and your workspace cheerful. Each piece is simple, durable, and easy to swap—so your setup can change as fast as your schedule.

Our kiosk concept keeps inventory light and choices clear: pick a charm, pick a color, grab a bundle. Whether you’re in a library booth or a lab, you’ll spend less time untangling and more time doing.

What we sell
  • Stallion / horseshoe cable charms
  • Cord keepers for backpacks and sleeves
  • Mini phone/ID stands
  • Sticker cable labels
