Project Overview: "Akaal Farms"

1) Project Overview

Working Title: Akaal Farms — Crops, Livestock & Ranch Showcase

Description & Purpose: A public-facing website for a local farmer, Sahibdeep Singh, to showcase his agricultural work — including wheat, rice, and corn production — as well as his ranch animals (cows, hens, and other domestic animals). The site improves his visibility in the community, gives buyers and neighbors a single place to see what he grows, and provides a simple way to contact him for visits, small orders, or collaboration. Because this course only allows read-only interactions, the site will simulate “orders/requests” with forms and client-side validation, not with a full database.

Intended Users

  • Local buyers (grocery shops, restaurants, neighbors)
  • Community members interested in fresh produce
  • Friends/family who want to see his ranch and animals
  • Potential partners (feed suppliers, transport, ag extension)

Content Overview

  • About the farmer & the ranch
  • Crops grown (wheat, rice, corn, seasonal items)
  • Livestock & domestic animals (cows, hens, other)
  • Photo gallery of fields, harvest, animals
  • Visit / Contact / “Request produce” form
  • Location & farm hours (if public)

Note: Client can later provide real photos and exact crop list — for now we use placeholders.

5) Interactivity Plan (Read-Only & Client-Safe)

  • jQuery UI
  • Lightbox gallery
  • AJAX (GET only)
  • Form validation
  • Interactive Crop List — simple JS filter to show crops by season (e.g. “All / Rabi / Kharif / Year-round”) on the “Crops & Products” page.
  • Livestock Gallery — thumbnails of the ranch animals that open in a lightbox. (Gallery page)
  • Availability Widget — small AJAX fetch of a local data/availability.json file that lists what's currently available. (Crops page)
  • Visit / Request Form — client-side validation for name, email/phone, and reason for contact. No database; after submit, show a “thank you” message or use mailto:. (Visit/Contact page)
  • FAQ Accordion — “Do you sell milk?”, “Can I visit?”, “What time are you open?” done with jQuery UI accordion. (Contact or About page)

No CRUD/back-end storage is used. All data loads are read-only from local JSON files under /project/data/.

8) Dynamic Functionality Details & References

  • availability.json (read-only) — Loaded via fetch() to populate the Crops page with current or typical availability (e.g. “wheat — Nov–Jan”).
  • Lightbox — For ranch and crop photos; makes animal pictures look nicer without leaving the page.
  • jQuery UI Accordion — For “Frequently Asked Questions” like “Do you sell milk?”, “Can I buy small quantity?”, “Can I visit on weekends?”
  • Simple calculator (optional) — JS to estimate cost for a crop based on quantity; runs on the Crops page only, client-side.
